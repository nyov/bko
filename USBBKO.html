Creating BKO Enabled USB

This page is still under construction, so just general directions are here and not detailed instructions.
We are working on creating detailed general instructions.
  1. Back-up the data from your USB.
  2. Follow HOWTO for how to make USB bootable.
  3. Install syslinux.
  4. Download syslinux.cfg and gpxe.krln from this site, and copy it into USB.
  5. Copy back the data that you had backed up.
